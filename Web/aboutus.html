About Us

Haukai Restaurant specializes in Māori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands. It was started by a couple (Mr Dion Paul & Mrs Susan Paul) who are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourists visiting the area.

The restaurant is only open in the evenings at the moment, from Wednesday to Sunday (5pm to 10pm) inclusive.

Mr & Mrs Paul also want thier customers to experience Te Ao Māori with the use of Karakia for blessing food and other Māori culture elements.

The dinner menu features Māori and Pākeha kai options and drinks. Click here Menu for the current restaurant menu.
